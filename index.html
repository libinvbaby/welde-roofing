About Us

Welde Engineering Solutions Pvt. Ltd. with an expertise of over 25 years in the field of roofing and special structure fabrication offers quality projects in affordable price ranges. We are executing projects for residential, commercial & industrial requirements with the support of experienced engineers and quality workers in a specified time by ensuring advanced quality inspection mechanisms. To execute a project without losing the valuable time & money of our customers we are assigning the engineering assessment program absolutely free of cost for finding the most appropriate solution within the budget. We are specialized in delivering all kinds of metal/non-metal roofing for factory premises, warehouses, divine centres, sports hubs, residences, car sheds, cattle sheds etc. We are one among the pioneers among the roofing contractors who use advanced technology in assessment, designing, drawing, executing & quality inspection. We have well equipped installation & service units all across Kerala for meeting all the service related enquiries within 24 hours. Welde Engineering is associating with the most reputed as well as trusted manufacturers in the World so that the materials and accessories we use always meet the top of the quality
